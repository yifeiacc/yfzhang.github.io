Research Interests

My research generally focuses on developing machine learning algorithms applied to graph-structured data. I'm also interested in deep generative models for graphs and privacy-preserved machine learning. Prior to my PhD, I have worked as Senior R&D Engineer at Alibaba Group and JD.com for two years
